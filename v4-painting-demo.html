FAQ

Common Questions

How long does a typical interior paint job take?

+

Most rooms take 1 day including prep. A whole-home interior (3-4 bedrooms, living areas, hallways) typically takes 3-5 days. We always give you a firm timeline before we start.

Do I need to move my furniture?

+

Nope. We move and cover everything. All furniture, floors, and fixtures are fully protected with drop cloths and plastic sheeting. We put everything back when we're done.

What paint brands do you use?

+

We primarily use Sherwin-Williams and Benjamin Moore. For budget-conscious projects, we offer Behr Premium. We'll recommend the right product for your surfaces and budget during the free consultation.

What does the 3-year warranty cover?

+

Peeling, bubbling, cracking, or fading on any surface we painted. If it fails, we fix it free. We also do a 30-day post-project check-in and leave a touch-up kit with your exact colors.

Can you help me pick colors?

+

Absolutely — every project includes a free color consultation. We bring large swatches, help you coordinate with your flooring, furniture, and lighting, and can even do test patches on your walls before committing.
