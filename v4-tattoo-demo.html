Common Questions

BEFORE YOUR SESSION

How do I book a consultation?

+

Fill out the form below or DM us on Instagram. Tell us what you want, where on your body, and approximate size. We get back to you within 24 hours.

Do you require a deposit?

+

Yes — $50 non-refundable deposit to book your session. It gets applied directly to your final cost. This secures your slot and covers the custom design work.

How should I prepare for my appointment?

+

Eat well, stay hydrated, wear comfortable clothing that gives access to the area. No alcohol 24 hours before. Get a good night’s sleep.

Can I see the design before my session?

+

Absolutely. We send your custom design 2–3 days before. Revisions are included — we don’t start until you’re 100% happy with it.

What about aftercare?

+

Free aftercare kit with every session plus detailed instructions. We’re always available if you have questions during healing.
