FAQ

Common Questions

How is pricing calculated?

+

Local moves are flat-rate based on home size and services. Long-distance moves are based on weight and mileage. We give you a binding quote after a free in-home or virtual survey — no surprises on moving day.

Are you licensed and insured?

+

Yes — fully. USDOT licensed, bonded, and insured with $1M in liability coverage. Every move includes basic valuation protection, and full replacement value coverage is available.

How far in advance should I book?

+

2-4 weeks is ideal for local moves. Long-distance moves should be booked 4-6 weeks out. End of month and weekends fill up fast — weekday moves usually have more availability and better rates.

Do I need to empty drawers and disassemble furniture?

+

Dresser drawers can stay full for local moves (we wrap the whole piece). We handle all furniture disassembly and reassembly — beds, desks, dining tables, shelving units. It's included.

What if something gets damaged?

+

We carry full insurance and offer valuation coverage on every move. If something is damaged (it's rare — less than 0.3% claim rate), we file immediately and resolve within 30 days. No runaround.
