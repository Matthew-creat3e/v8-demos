FAQ

Common Questions

Everything you need to know before getting a fence installed.

How much does a fence cost?▼

Chain link starts around $12/ft installed, wood $28-55/ft, vinyl $25-50/ft, and iron $55-100/ft. Use our cost estimator above for a quick ballpark based on your specs.

How long does installation take?▼

Most residential fences install in 1-3 days. Chain link can be done in a single day. Commercial projects may take 3-5 days.

Do I need a permit?▼

Most KC metro cities require a fence permit. We handle the entire permit process — application, fees, and inspection scheduling. It's included.

What about underground utilities?▼

We call 811 before every job to mark all underground utilities. Required by law and we never skip it.

Which fence is best for dogs?▼

For diggers: 6ft wood with buried kickboard. For jumpers: 6ft vinyl or chain link. We also add dig guards and self-closing gates.

Do you offer financing?▼

Yes. Flexible payment plans through our financing partner. Most approvals take under 5 minutes with rates as low as 0% for qualified buyers.

What's your warranty?▼

Lifetime workmanship warranty on all installations. Materials carry manufacturer warranty (typically 20-lifetime years).

Can you remove my old fence?▼

Yes. Old fence removal and disposal is included in most quotes. We haul away all debris and leave the site clean.
