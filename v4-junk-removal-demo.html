Common Questions

GOT QUESTIONS?

HOW QUICKLY CAN YOU COME OUT?

+

Most jobs are same-day or next-day. Call us before noon and there's a good chance we'll be there by 5pm. We work 7 days a week.

WHAT DOES IT COST?

+

Pricing is based on how much space your items take in our truck. Small loads start at $99, most jobs run $249-$449. We give you an exact price on-site before we start — no surprises.

DO I NEED TO BE HOME?

+

Nope. As long as we can access the items, we can handle it. Just point us in the right direction and we'll take care of the rest. We'll text you when we're done.

WHAT HAPPENS TO MY STUFF?

+

We donate usable items to local charities, recycle what we can, and responsibly dispose of the rest. About 60% of what we haul gets a second life.

DO YOU TAKE HAZARDOUS MATERIALS?

+

We can't haul chemicals, paint, oil, or batteries. But we take almost everything else — furniture, appliances, electronics, yard waste, construction debris, hot tubs, you name it.
