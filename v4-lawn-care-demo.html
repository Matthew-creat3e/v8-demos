Common Questions

HAVE QUESTIONS?

Do I need to sign a contract?

+

No contracts. Ever. You can cancel anytime with a quick text. Most clients stay because they want to, not because they have to.

What if it rains on my mow day?

+

We reschedule to the next dry day at no extra charge. You’ll get a text letting you know the new time. Your yard never gets skipped.

Do you bring your own equipment?

+

Yes — commercial-grade mowers, trimmers, edgers, and blowers. We maintain our equipment daily so your lawn gets a clean, sharp cut every time.

How do I pay?

+

We send an invoice after each service. Pay online, by card, or by check — whatever’s easiest. Monthly billing is also available.

What areas do you serve?

+

We cover Kansas City, Overland Park, Lee’s Summit, Independence, Liberty, Blue Springs, and surrounding areas. If you’re within 30 miles of downtown KC, we’ve got you.
