Common Questions

Frequently Asked

How much does lawn mowing cost in Independence?

+

Most residential yards in Independence run $30–$55 per visit depending on size and complexity. Use our instant quote tool above for an exact estimate — no obligation, no waiting.

How often should I mow my lawn?

+

Weekly during the Kansas City growing season (April–October) keeps your lawn thick and healthy. We offer bi-weekly plans too. Never cut more than one-third of the blade height at once.

Do I need to sign a contract?

+

No contracts. Ever. You can cancel anytime with a quick text. Most clients stay because they want to, not because they have to.

What if it rains on my service day?

+

We reschedule to the next dry day at no extra charge. You'll get a text letting you know the new time. Your yard never gets skipped.

Do you do tree removal or just trimming?

+

We handle trimming, pruning, and stump grinding. For full tree removal on large trees, we partner with a licensed arborist and coordinate the whole job for you.

What areas do you serve?

+

We serve Independence, Blue Springs, Lee's Summit, Kansas City, Grain Valley, Raytown, Liberty, and surrounding areas. If you're within 30 miles of Independence, we've got you covered.
