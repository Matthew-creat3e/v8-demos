Common Questions

HAVE QUESTIONS?

Do you charge for diagnostics?

+

No. We run a full scan, explain what we find, and give you a written estimate — all free. You only pay if you approve the work.

How long do most repairs take?

+

Most common services (brakes, oil, tires) are same-day. Bigger jobs like engine or transmission work typically take 1–3 days. We’ll give you a timeline upfront.

Do you offer loaner cars?

+

Yes — free loaner vehicles are available for major repairs that take more than a day. Just ask when you drop off.

What’s your warranty?

+

12 months or 12,000 miles on all parts and labor. If something we fixed doesn’t hold up, bring it back — we make it right at no charge.

Do you work on all makes and models?

+

We service all domestic and foreign vehicles — cars, trucks, SUVs, and vans. Diesel too. If it has an engine, we work on it.
